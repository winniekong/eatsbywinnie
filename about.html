About

Hello, I'm Winnie!

This website was created to inspire, and to reignite the passion for baking. Due to COVID-19, I had to abruptly leave my dorm and move back home with my family. Since then, baking has become our favorite family bonding activity as well as a learning experience.

It all started with my mom's failed attempt at making a rice cooker sponge cake, which inspired my dad to finally buy an electric hand mixer. However, baking truly became a part of our nightly routine when New York City entered a statewide lockdown. We started our new adventure, attempting to bake different types of dessert and experimenting with traditional American flavors by adding an Asian twist to them.
